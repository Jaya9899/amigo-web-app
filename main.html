The Problem

Why students
stay silent.

😰
Fear of Judgement
Students avoid asking doubts in class because they fear being judged by peers or looking unintelligent in front of the faculty.
Student Pain
🤐
Hesitation to Interrupt
During a flowing lecture, students don't want to break the faculty's momentum — so genuine doubts go unasked and unresolved.
Student Pain
🔁
Repetitive Questions
Faculty often receive the same question from multiple students, wasting precious class time answering the same thing repeatedly.
Faculty Pain
📉
No Engagement Visibility
Faculty have no real-time insight into whether students are understanding the material — they only find out during exams, when it's too late.
Faculty Pain
How Amigo Solves It

Powerful features,
zero disruption.

🙋
Anonymous Doubt Posting
Students post doubts in a fully anonymous chat during live lectures. No names, no fear — just honest questions that reach the faculty.
Students
🤖
AI Content Filtering
Every message passes through AI moderation before reaching the faculty. Irrelevant, abusive, or spam messages are automatically filtered out.
AI-Powered
🧩
Smart Doubt Clustering
Doubts with similar context are grouped together automatically — so the faculty addresses each topic once instead of repeating answers.
AI-Powered
📝
Live Polls & Quizzes
Faculty can launch instant polls and quizzes mid-lecture to gauge comprehension in real time and adjust their teaching accordingly.
Faculty
📊
AI-Generated Session Analytics
After every session, Amigo generates a detailed analytics report for faculty — doubt trends, participation rates, comprehension heatmaps, and more.
Post-Session
🔒
Complete Anonymity Guarantee
Student identities are never revealed to the faculty or other students. The system is designed so anonymity cannot be bypassed — ever.
Privacy-First
How It Works

Seamless
during every
lecture.

During a live session, students open Amigo and type their doubts anonymously. The AI filters out noise, clusters similar questions, and presents clean, organized doubts to the faculty in real time — without ever breaking the flow of the lecture.

✍️Student types a doubt anonymously during the lecture
🛡️AI filters abusive & irrelevant messages instantly
🧩Similar doubts are auto-clustered — no repetition for faculty
📋Faculty sees clean, grouped doubts & launches polls to check understanding
📊After the session, AI generates a full analytics report
